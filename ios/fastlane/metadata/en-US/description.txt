Project Brain helps you stay on track with personalised coaching support from Dot+Dash.

Connect with your coach, set goals, chat with text and voice messages, and get the support you need—wherever you are.

Features:
• Secure sign-in and account management
• Coach messaging with photos, voice notes, and files
• Goal tracking and progress updates
• Push notifications for new messages and reminders
• Subscription management via your web account

Project Brain is designed for people who benefit from structured, compassionate coaching support.
